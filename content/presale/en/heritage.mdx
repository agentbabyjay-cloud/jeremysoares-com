## About L'Héritage

L'Héritage is a landmark residential project in the heart of Outremont. 28 meticulously designed residences set the new standard for luxury living in Montreal.

### Key Features

- 28 exclusive residences: 2-bedroom, 3-bedroom, and penthouse collections
- Rooftop pool with panoramic views of Outremont and Mont-Royal
- Private spa, sauna, and wellness centre
- 24/7 concierge service
- Private porte-cochère entrance
- Wine cellar and private dining room
- Fully equipped gym
- Steps from Outremont's best restaurants, cafés, and cultural venues

### Pricing

Residences start at $695,000 and range to $2,400,000 for penthouse collection.

## About L'Émeraude

L'Émeraude brings a curated collection of 42 contemporary residences to one of Montreal's most desirable neighbourhoods. Designed for those who value design, walkability, and community.

### Key Features

- 42 residences: studios, 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, and penthouse units
- Floor-to-ceiling windows with sweeping Plateau views
- Premium kitchen packages: quartz countertops, integrated appliances
- Private terraces on select units
- Secure underground parking
- Steps from Mont-Royal Avenue, parks, restaurants, and the metro

### Pricing

Units start at $485,000 for studios and range to $1,200,000+ for penthouse residences.

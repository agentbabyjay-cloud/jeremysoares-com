## Le Botanik — Griffintown

A boutique 120-unit development by Devmont Group in one of Montreal's fastest-growing neighbourhoods. Floor plans range from studios to three-bedroom penthouses, with ground-floor commercial space anchoring the streetscape.

### Key Features

- 12 storeys, LEED-certified design
- Rooftop terrace with panoramic city views
- Ground-floor retail and café
- Steps from the Lachine Canal bike path
- Walking distance to ETS, Lucien-L'Allier metro
